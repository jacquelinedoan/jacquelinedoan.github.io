About Me

I first became fastinated with mathematics when I was in a first-year introductory calculus class. I was a media studies student at the time, so it was a big change for me. I was able to find ways to integrate (hah!) my creative side and my busy schedule as a mathematics student. In my free time, I am an illustrator, a painter, and a graphic designer. One of my goals as a future academic is to ultilize my design background in order to make mathematics accessible and interesting.
